Coatings failure Investigation

The use of a protective coating involves multiple factors, requiring the skills of skilled inspectors to recognize and ascertain the underlying reasons for any failures.

The study includes gathering information on the type of coating, method of application, lifespan, working environment, and physical characteristics of the coating that failed.

With this data, Rig Marine Solutions will produce a Technical Report that adheres to (Rule 35) Expert Witness criteria.
