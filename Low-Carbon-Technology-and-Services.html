Ultrasonic Bolt Inspection

Our skilled team provides Ultrasonic Bolt Inspections utilizing sonar ultrasonic time-of-flight (TOF) measurement tools that measure, show, save, and transfer bolt load and elongation information in a dedicated unit.

Rig Marine Solutions provides a strong, certified option; through a simple ultrasonic inspection of current bolts, one technician can swiftly assess their state and remove any need for hydraulic retightening.

Solar Hybrid Tower Lighting

We offer innovative solar-powered light towers that enhance the sustainability of client job sites. The HiLight S2+ is an innovative solar-powered LED light tower designed for ease of use, allowing users to cut CO2 emissions by as much as six tons in relation to conventional technologies.
