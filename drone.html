Drone (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) Services

Rig Marine Solutions offers cutting-edge Drone (Unmanned Aerial Vehicle) services to conduct secure, efficient, and thorough inspections of inaccessible locations. Our UAV technology enables detailed visual evaluations of structures like pipelines, towers, and offshore platforms, reducing the requirement for scaffolding or shutdowns.

  • Project / Site Flyovers, Progress Imagery
  • Site Conditioning monitoring
  • Pre- Shutdown Site Assessment, Defects Identification
  • Aerial Thermal Imaging, Flare Tips Inspection

Project / Site Flyovers, Progress Imagery

Our UAV technology allows us to deliver real-time, detailed perspectives of construction sites, infrastructure initiatives, and large-scale developments, assisting clients in tracking progress, evaluating site conditions, and making informed choices. Through the provision of comprehensive aerial imagery and video, we boost project clarity, enhance safety measures, and simplify reporting procedures. These flyovers provide an economical, effective method to monitor advancement and guarantee that projects remain on time and satisfy necessary criteria.

Site Conditioning monitoring

Our drones gather high-resolution images and information, allowing us to monitor environmental shifts, evaluate site conditions, and recognize potential hazards. This effective and unobtrusive approach enables regular monitoring without interfering with operations. Our UAV services provide precise insights into site conditions, enabling clients to uphold safety, enhance project planning, and meet industry standards, while also lowering costs and time linked to conventional monitoring approaches.

Pre- Shutdown Site Assessment, Defects Identification

Our drones enable us to securely inspect extensive or hard-to-reach locations, detecting possible flaws or troubling areas prior to an outage. This proactive strategy assists clients in tackling key challenges, organizing maintenance efficiently, and reducing expensive downtime. Through high-resolution imaging and precise data gathering, our UAV services guarantee comprehensive evaluations, facilitating accurate defect detection and well-informed decision-making to maintain seamless operations.

Aerial Thermal Imaging, Flare Tips Inspection

Our drones feature thermal cameras that identify heat irregularities in flare systems, pipelines, and other high-temperature machinery, allowing for the early detection of possible problems such as leaks or inefficiencies. By examining flare tips from above, we lower safety hazards and decrease the necessity for shutdowns or scaffolding. This technology guarantees precise, immediate data gathering, enabling prompt maintenance and improved operational safety.
