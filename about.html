About Us

Rig Marine Solutions focuses on providing comprehensive inspection assistance designed to cater to the requirements of various sectors, such as construction, manufacturing, local governments, railways, highways, and oil and gas.

Our team of highly qualified and experienced Inspectors is committed to offering an extensive range of services.

This encompasses document and specification evaluations, dimensional assessments, protective coatings, insulation inspections and surveys, failure analysis, quality control inspections, and NDT support services, guaranteeing that your quality plan meets the highest standards and specifications.

We take great pride in our careful attention to detail, precision, and steadfast dedication to providing trustworthy and impartial inspection reports. Through close collaboration with our clients, we guarantee that their projects benefit from optimal oversight and quality control.

Select Rig Marine Solutions for reliable, skilled expertise in NDT, Painting, and Insulation Inspection Services.
