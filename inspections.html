Renewables

We collaborate with our clients from the initial evaluation of the structure's coating system and corrosion analysis, delivering specifications that satisfy both environmental standards and the client's desired design lifespan.

In-person evaluation is essential to guarantee that the designated design lifespan is met. This includes confirming adequate surface preparation and coating application. Our thorough examination and testing also involve assessing chloride salt levels, which can significantly affect the coating system, making sure all surface preparation criteria are fulfilled.

Get in touch to find out more

Petro-chemical

Petrochemical paint inspection services are crucial for ensuring the safety and integrity of structures and equipment in the petrochemical sector.

These services consist of comprehensive evaluations of coatings on pipelines, tanks, and facilities to identify and prevent corrosion, chemical deterioration, and other types of harm. Specialized inspectors evaluate the state of the paint, confirming it aligns with industry standards and requirements.

Get in touch to find out more

Maritime

Carrying out coating inspections and surveys in the commercial shipping sector requires significant expertise and thorough knowledge to guarantee high quality and provide precise data for maintenance needs.

Our experienced team works closely with shipping companies and shipyards to improve the effectiveness of coating systems. Through careful inspections at each stage, we guarantee that surface preparation and coating application comply with the necessary standards and specifications.

Local Authority

By working alongside local governments, including the councils of the City of Aktau, we have gained a thorough understanding of budget limitations.
